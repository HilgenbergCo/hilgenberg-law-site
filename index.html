About

Hilgenberg Law LLC was founded in 2026 by attorney Scott Hilgenberg. He is licensed in Oregon and Washington and focuses on land use, real estate, environmental, and regulatory matters. His background combines government service, public policy advocacy, appellate land use litigation, and transactional commercial real estate experience.

Scott has worked extensively within Oregon's land use system, including as Staff Attorney for the Oregon Land Use Board of Appeals and Chair of the Oregon State Bar's Real Estate and Land Use Section. More recently, he served as in-house counsel for a commercial real estate brokerage and development company, advising on acquisitions, leasing, development, property management, and land use matters.

He has advised governments, Fortune 500 companies, real estate developers, property owners, financial institutions, and public interest organizations. Representative matters include land use appeals, development approvals, zoning and permitting issues, real estate transactions, easements, commercial leasing, environmental compliance, and local government proceedings.

Background

Scott is a third-generation real estate professional and graduate of Lewis & Clark Law School, where he earned a Juris Doctor and Certificate in Environmental and Natural Resources Law. He is a member of the Cornelius Honor Society and recipient of the Larry Kressel Memorial Scholarship for excellence in land use law.

Scott works remotely from his hometown of Green Bay, Wisconsin. He is also a Wisconsin commercial real estate broker, yoga practitioner, and avid hiker.
